Frequently Asked Questions

What is 1MChart?

1MChart is an educational trading analytics platform that provides comprehensive tools for analyzing Nasdaq market data, studying trading patterns, and developing trading strategies through research and education.

Is 1MChart free to use?

Yes! 1MChart is completely free to use with full features. Simply sign up for free to unlock all premium features including complete gap analysis, backtest tools, and market simulator. You can also try our sample features without signing up.

Do you provide investment advice?

No, 1MChart is designed exclusively for educational and research purposes. We do not provide investment advice, guarantee profits, or predict market outcomes. All information is for educational use only.

What data do you provide?

We provide comprehensive Nasdaq market data including 1-minute candlestick charts, gap analysis, earnings data, and economic event analysis from 2015 to present.

How accurate is your market data?

We source our data from reliable financial data providers and strive for accuracy. However, we do not guarantee real-time accuracy and recommend users verify critical information from multiple sources.

Can I use 1MChart for live trading?

1MChart is designed for educational and research purposes. While you can use our insights to inform your trading decisions, we strongly recommend using our platform as part of a comprehensive trading education strategy.

How do I get technical support?

For technical support, please email us at onemchart.onemchart@mail.com with a detailed description of your issue. Include screenshots if possible, and we'll respond within 24 hours during business days.

Can I cancel my account?

Yes, you can delete your account at any time through your account settings or by contacting our support team. Since 1MChart is completely free, there are no charges or fees to worry about.
