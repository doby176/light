The Power of Timing in Gap Trading

While gap size determines your strategy (fade vs. continuation), timing determines your success rate. Our analysis reveals that the same gap can have dramatically different outcomes based on when it occurs and how quickly it fills. Understanding these timing patterns is the key to consistent profitability.

Why Timing Matters

  • Fill Speed: Gaps filling in <30 minutes have 56.99% reversal rate vs. 10.47% for slow fills
  • Time of Day: 14:00 lows achieve 43.53% reversal rate vs. 26.19% at 10:00
  • Day of Week: Wednesday gaps achieve 42.72% reversal rate vs. 34.91% on Monday
  • Volume: High-volume gaps achieve 72.41% reversal rate vs. 35% average
