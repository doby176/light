Gap Analysis & Trading

What are the best gap sizes to trade?

Based on our analysis, the most profitable gap sizes are:

  • 0.5-1.0% gaps: Highest fill rates across all weekdays (up to 75.68% on Friday down gaps)
  • 1.0-1.5% gaps: Good fill rates, especially on Tuesdays (78.38% for Tuesday up gaps)
  • Avoid 3.0%+ gaps: Consistently fill less than 40% of the time

Key Insight: Small gaps (0.5-1.0%) consistently show the highest fill rates, suggesting a mean-reversion edge for intraday trading.

Why do down gaps fill more often than up gaps?

Our data shows that down gaps consistently have higher fill rates than up gaps. For example:

  • Monday down gaps (0.5-1.0%): 73.81% fill rate
  • Monday up gaps (0.5-1.0%): 51.79% fill rate

This pattern likely reflects:

  • Panic Selling: Investors often overreact to negative news at market open
  • Market Recovery: Prices tend to recover throughout the trading day
  • Institutional Buying: Large buyers often step in after initial selling pressure

Which day of the week is best for gap trading?

Tuesdays show the most consistent gap fill patterns:

  • Tuesday up gaps (1.0-1.5%): 78.38% fill rate
  • Tuesday down gaps (0.5-1.0%): 67.27% fill rate

This "Tuesday effect" may be due to:

  • Market participants adjusting positions after Monday's news
  • Midweek reversion behavior
  • Excess reaction correction from Monday events

Note: Thursday also shows interesting patterns, with down gaps (1.5-2.0%) filling at 88.24%.
